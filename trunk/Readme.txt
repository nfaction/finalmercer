===============================================================\
					Usage Guide:\
===============================================================\
-When you start the program, you will be welcomed by an introductions splash screen.\
-To create your own Rube Goldburg machine, \
	Click "File", then "Start", and you will be taken to your very own Sandbox.  \
You have the capability of saving and loading one previous state.  \
-You can create a your machine, by clicking on the object to be placed, moving your mouse to the position you would like to be placed, then click to place. \
(Hint: object will only be added to the model when another object is picked up from the toolbox and placed in the toolbox's empty space)\
- To remove or move an object, click on the object you wish to move or \
remove, then either place in new location in sandbox, or click on the empty space in the toolbox to remove it from the sandbox.  \
-To watch your Rube Goldburg machine run, click the "Start" button in the middle of the screen.\
(Hint: Once the machine is running, you will not be able to click any objects on the screen, until you reset or clear your Rube Goldburg machine)\
-To reset to the beginning of your created machine, click "File", then "Reset".\
-To save your state, click "Scenario", then "Save".\
- To Load your previous state, click "Scenario", then "Load".\
-You can also clear the sandbox to start over by clicking "File" then "Clear Sandbox"\
-To change the coefficient of gravity in your Rube Goldburg machine, click "Options", then "Change Gravity".  Type in the new value of gravity as a floating point positive number, then click "Submit".\
-To go back to the splash screen, click "File", then "Main Menu".\
-Close by clicking the "X" in the corner of your screen.\
